Grilled Cauliflower Steak

Description

Grilled Cauliflower Steak is a healthy, quick, and simple dinner that’s on the table in a flash!

Ingredients

  • Marinade – We used a blend of olive oil with garlic and onion powder, paprika for a bit of a kick, and liquid smoke to give it that smokey flavor. Add a splash of lemon juice or some fresh herbs if you’d like.
  • Cauliflower – Pick a large cauliflower head that’s fresh looking and free from blemishes. Frozen florets will also work, just thaw first & adjust the cooking time as needed!

Instructions

Cutting into steaks

  1. Cut each half into 1 1/2 – 2 inch wide “steaks”. You should be able to get two steaks from each half depending on the size of the cauliflower. If you cut thinner steaks the florets will not hold together.

  2. Cut the cauliflower steaks by FIRST cutting the head of the cauliflower in half down the center on a cutting board. Do not remove the stem.

Cooking on the grill

  1. Whisk the cauliflower steak marinade ingredients per recipe below.

  2. Brush both sides of the cauliflower with the seasoning and grill on a medium grill for 4-5 minutes per side or until tender. (Note: These also can be cooked in the oven).
